Comic book

For inspiration I used a fortress Old Castle which is a part of Old Town Varazdin. The architecture of the castle gave me an idea since every tower is designed a little different. If they were people, they would have different personalities. To make the comic fun, I decided that those personalities would be of the seven dwarfs (Grumpy, Happy, Sleepy, Bashful, Sneezy, Dopey and Doc).

Photo anarchy

This assignment was to create image anarchy on a topic of our choosing. I used Adobe Photoshop for raster graphics, and Freepik website - for finding the best available graphics to use.

Creative typography

This project was about creating a dynamic design using typography. My work shows typography letters emerging from a book and creating an image of a sailing ship, specifically Drakkar - Viking ship. This project took a lot of time and patience, as the position of every letter is important. For example, the sail of a ship is formatted out of letters for the word sail.

The whole idea was that while reading, letters create images in our minds.

Gif animation

For this assignment, we had complete freedom. As I didn't have any specific ideas, I started experimenting with clipping masks and as I love using animals figures in my works, I chose a wolf motif. Finally going through Photoshop features and tools, I discovered Photoshop animation and created animated snow.
